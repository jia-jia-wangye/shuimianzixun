MEDICAL PROBLEMS AFFECTING SLEEP

受调查者超过一半在过去3个月中晚上存在忧虑 (58%)及外部干扰因素(26%)而影响睡眠。睡眠欠佳的后果包括：疲乏 (46%)，情绪不稳定或易怒(41%)，消极(39%)或注意力难以集中(39%)。

More than half of the respondents had anxiety (58%) and external interference factors (26%) in the past three months. The consequences of poor sleep included fatigue (46%), emotional instability or irritability (41%), negative (39%) or difficulty in focusing (39%).

干扰睡眠的首因

PSYCHOLOGICAL STRESS IS THE FIRST CAUSE OF
SLEEP DISTURBANCE

《白皮书》显示：近 80% 的人都比较关注自己的睡眠，心理压力大、工作学习任务重是影响睡眠的两大重要因素，仅有 30% 的人睡醒后感觉精力满满，大多数人在睡醒后都有一些不适。
According to the white paper, nearly 80% of people pay more attention to their sleep. Psychological pressure and heavy work and study tasks are two important factors affecting sleep. Only 30% of people feel full of energy after waking up, and most people have some discomfort after waking up.

各代际失眠原因迥异

THE CAUSES OF INSOMNIA VARY FROM
GENERATION TO GENERATION

调查显示：00后主要失眠原因来自于学业压力;作为社会中坚力量的70后，80后和90后，工作压力过大是其失眠的主要原因；60后没有原因，莫名失眠；50后失眠原因受气候和生理原因影响较大。
The survey shows that: the main cause of insomnia after 00 comes from academic pressure; as the backbone of the society, the Post-70s, 80s and 90s, excessive work pressure is the main reason for their insomnia; after 60, there is no reason, inexplicable insomnia; the reason of insomnia after 50 is greatly affected by climate and physiological reasons.

THE AVERAGE SLEEP TIME WAS 7.5 HOURS
BELOW HEALTH STANOAROS

90后睡眠时间平均值为7.5小时，低于健康睡眠时间，六成以上觉得睡眠时间不足。其中43.3%睡眠时间为7-8小时，23.4%睡眠时间为8-10小时。同时，调查结果显示，30.9%的人入睡需要30分钟以上，0.9%需要药物助眠才能入睡。
The average sleep time of the post-90s was 7.5 hours, which was lower than that of healthy sleep. At the same time, the survey results showed that 30.9% of the people needed more than 30 minutes to fall asleep, and 0.9% needed drugs to help them sleep.
